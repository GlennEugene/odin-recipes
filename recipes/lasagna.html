Lasagna

Description

Lasagna recipe for 2 or more people.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large sweet onion, chopped
  • 1/2 pound sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Stir in the onion, mushrooms, garlic, and tomato sauce. Cook and stir until the sauce is thickened.
  2. Drain the beef and add it to the sauce. Stir in the cheese and any additional ingredients. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Serve immediately.
