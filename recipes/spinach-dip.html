Spinach Dip

Description

Spinach Dip recipe for 4 or more people.

Ingredients

  • 1 box frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 packet onion soup mix
  • Freshly cracked black pepper, to taste

Steps

In a large bowl, combine the spinach, sour cream, mayonnaise, onion soup mix, and pepper. Mix well
